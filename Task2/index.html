About us

Our developers are a bunch of young, talented individuals full of innovative ideas and are always ready for new challenges. Offer

Mobile applications

While creating mobile applications we use various technologies and outstanding tools. Libraries and modules developed at Software Mansion are used by thousands of React Native and Expo professionals in creation of their products. We are always open to new opportunities to use cutting-edge technologies and tackle exciting challenges.

Web applications

We develop custom web applications for startups and existing businesses alike. We are experienced in a variety of technologies and are comfortable with development of projects ranging from a couple of weeks to dozens of man-months.

Modern multimedia solutions

We have extensive experience with building modern multimedia solutions. We offer consulting and development services in, among others, the following areas: audio/video real-time streaming and processing, mass storage, performance and scalability.

Open source contribution

At Software Mansion we strongly believe that our commitment in Open-Sourced products is important. Thousands of developers have already used our React Native modules in production.

Education

Startup development

Start-ups were our focus since the start of Software Mansion. We offer consulting, design, development and analysis services for startups in all stages of growth. We are also involved in several start-ups as cofounders.
